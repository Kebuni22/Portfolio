Projects

I am actively involved in multiple projects as part of my academic pursuits.

Resturent Managemen System (RAD Group Project)

In our project, we embarked on the creation of a Restaurant Management System. This ambitious project was developed using Visual Studio and C#, with MySQL as the backend database. The system was designed to streamline restaurant operations, from order management to inventory tracking, enhancing efficiency and customer service. It was an excellent opportunity to apply our programming skills in a practical context and gain hands-on experience in software development for the hospitality industry.

Mobile Phone Repairing Shop Management System

In our project, we crafted a comprehensive website and admin interface for a mobile phone repairing shop system. Our development stack included PHP, MySQL, and HTML. This system enables seamless management of repair requests, inventory tracking, and customer communication. It was a valuable experience in web development, allowing us to apply our skills to a real-world scenario and provide an effective solution for mobile phone repair businesses.

Visual Computing Group Project

For our Visual Computing group project, we embarked on an extensive multimedia campaign for a travel company called "The Ceylon Voyage." We utilized various Adobe software tools such as Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Premiere Pro, and Flash to create a dynamic online presence. Our project included the development of a website, engaging Facebook and Instagram pages, a captivating magazine, and eye-catching posters. By combining our creative and technical skills, we successfully promoted The Ceylon Voyage, making it an enticing destination for travelers seeking unforgettable experiences.

Home Automation System (IOT)

In our project, we designed a home automation system using IoT technology. Leveraging an Arduino board, LED bulbs, and custom circuits, we created a smart home solution that allows users to control and automate various aspects of their home environment. This project exemplifies the potential of IoT in enhancing convenience and energy efficiency within households.
